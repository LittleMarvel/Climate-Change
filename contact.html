Ways To Help

What Can You Do?

You can do the following to beat climate change-

  • Urge government to take bold, ambitious climate action now.
  • Use energy wisely and save money too!
  • Encourage the use of renewable energies.
  • Get your electricity from the wind and the sun.
  • Recycle your clothes.
  • Take public transit.

Mail The People In Power

Climate change has started altering the earth's environment, resulting in many recent natural disasters. This message is intended to make the people in power aware of where we stand today in regards to climate change and the environment. Many countries are suffering for a number of reasons, including farming, transportation, power plants and more. In order to limit the worst effects of climate change, countries have only a limited time to act.


Educate the people in charge in your location regarding the changes occurring in our climatic environment so that they can take action.
